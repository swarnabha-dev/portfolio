PROJECT: HISTOPATHOLOGY IMAGE REGISTRATION

This project focuses on leveraging Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) implemented with TensorFlow to enhance image recognition capabilities. The methodology involves preprocessing and augmenting image datasets, constructing CNN architectures with TensorFlow APIs, and optimizing hyperparameters for improved performance.

The results showcase significant advancements in image classification tasks, surpassing previous benchmarks and achieving remarkable accuracy rates. Visualization techniques are employed to gain insights into the network's decision-making process, paving the way for innovative solutions in computer vision.
